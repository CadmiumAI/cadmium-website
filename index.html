Built for piping engineers

Every feature designed around your actual workflow.

Automatic pipe detection

Cadmium analyzes 3D solid geometry to identify every pipe run, fitting, and connection point in your model. No manual selection needed.

Smart dimensioning

Dimension lines placed automatically with correct measurements. Aligned, rotated, and offset — all calculated from the 3D model data.

Component recognition

Automatically classifies valves, elbows, flanges, tees, reducers, and more. Each component tagged with its spec and description.

Network mapping

Builds a complete connectivity graph of your piping system. See exactly how every component connects, with weld points identified.

Interactive 3D review

Explore your piping model in a browser-based 3D viewer. Click components for details, filter by type, and verify connections visually.

Export anywhere

Generate production-ready DXF files that open natively in AutoCAD. Also export to PDF, SVG, and PNG for documentation and review.
